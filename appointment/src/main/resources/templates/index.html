Our Best Services

Mobile Services

When you have a new-born baby or a loved one who is immobile, taking them to a hospital for medical tests can be as traumatic for them as it is for you. At ABC we understand your concerns better than anyone else. This is why we offer mobile services that bring our very best healthcare professionals right to you.

Collection Centres

At ABC Hospitals we are proud of our outstanding record for performing state-of-the-art medical investigations. To share our expertise with our consumers and medical professionals beyond Colombo we have worked hard to maintain an efficient network of sample collection centres and Satellite Laboratories throughout Sri Lanka. This extensive network allows patients and medical professionals from across the country to have access to our world-class laboratory infrastructure and services.

Preventive Health Checks

‘Prevention is better than cure,’ or so the saying goes – we couldn’t agree more. This is why at ABC Preventive we offer you a range of health checks that are designed to detect any early warning signs for some of the more common but preventable illnesses such as diabetes, hypertension or cholesterol. Early detection and early treatment also gives you better odds against more serious illnesses such as cancer.
